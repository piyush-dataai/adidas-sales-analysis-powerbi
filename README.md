Adidas Sales Analytics Dashboard
Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze Adidas sales performance across products, regions, customers, retailers, and time periods. The dashboard helps stakeholders monitor key business KPIs, identify sales trends, and uncover actionable insights for decision-making.

Business Objective

The objective of this project is to:

Analyze Adidas sales performance from 2020–2022.
Track revenue, profit, and order trends.
Identify top-performing products and regions.
Understand customer and retailer behavior.
Support data-driven business decisions.

Key Insights
Lifestyle emerged as the highest-performing product category.
West region generated the strongest overall business performance.
Profit margin remained close to 30%, indicating healthy profitability.
Q4 showed the highest sales activity.
Sales were distributed relatively evenly across customer segments.
Foot Locker, Amazon, Walmart, and Target were among the top-performing retailers.
